A Bit About Me



My name is Constance. I was born in the country of Nigeria. The first day I learned about software engineering was when I was 14-15 years old in my engineering class. Right there I decided I wanted to be a software engineer. But I lost courage shortly after because I thought I wasn't smart enough. Good thing I don't listen to the negative voice in my head.
